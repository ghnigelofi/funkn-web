What's up?

 

Got questions?
The answer might be just here.

 
 
Funkn. added wrong songs from my Spotify playlist.
Bots make mistakes. A LOT.
Funkn.'s Spotify to YouTube converstion isn't a 100% accurate.
 
Funkn. is offline. What to do?
Funkn. takes a nap too. We can't provide a free, premium music experience without pauses.
If Funkn. is to be online 24/7, we need funding. And you know, no one really likes paying for a service that could be free.
 
Why is Funkn. not playing anything?
There are four instances:
1. Funkn. is server muted. You can manually unmute Funkn. via Discord settings.
2. Funkn.'s permissions are incomplete. Make sure Funkn. has the SPEAK, CONNECT, and SEND MESSAGES permissions.
3. Funkn. is offline and is taking an idle. Every last 5 days of the months, Funkn. takes a break from free services.
4. Funkn. is offline and is getting an update. Expect better performances!
 
Funkn. doesn't play my SoundCloud playlist...
Funkn. supports Spotify playlists (to YouTube) in version v4, but not SoundCloud playlists. yet.
But it supports Spotify TO YouTube equivalent, and SoundCloud songs. Funkn. will always search for YouTube videos. Input a Spotify link or a SoundCloud link to play them.
 
Funkn. plays distorted/glitching audio.
There are two instances:
1. Discord servers are slow. Change your server region to test other servers.
2. Funkn.'s VPS is slow. If the API Ping is more than 600ms, it is likely that the audio will glitch.
